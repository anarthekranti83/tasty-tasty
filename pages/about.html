About Food

Food is an important part of our daily life. It gives us energy and helps our body grow and stay healthy. Good food keeps us strong and active.

Why Food Is Important

Healthy food provides nutrients like vitamins, minerals, and proteins. Eating fresh and balanced food helps prevent diseases and improves overall health.

Types of Food

  • Vegetables and Fruits
  • Grains and Cereals
  • Dairy Products
  • Proteins (Pulses, Eggs, Meat)

Choosing healthy food every day is a good habit and helps us live a better life.
